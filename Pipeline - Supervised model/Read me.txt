DESCRIPTION:
This script performs the initial supervised training of a U-Net architecture 
to segment material microstructures into five distinct classes: Porosity, Gel, 
Aggregates, Unreacted phases, and Impurities.

CORE FUNCTIONALITY:
1. DATA LOADING: Processes 512x512 image patches and corresponding labels 
   from local directories.
2. PERFORMANCE METRICS: Implements a custom MeanIoU metric (MeanIoU_custom) 
   to monitor multi-class segmentation accuracy.


OUTPUTS:
- supervised_model.keras: The trained weights used as the baseline for SSL.
- Training plots: Visualization of IoU and loss curves.

REQUIREMENTS:
- TensorFlow 2.x
- Keras
- OpenCV
- Scikit-Learn
- Matplotlib
